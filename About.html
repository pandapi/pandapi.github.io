Hyacinth is a freelance journalist and videographer based in Athens, Georgia.

She is a second-year student in the health and medical journalism program at the University of Georgia, who currently writes health stories in Jackson County.

Her main interests are making videos, writing stories about scientific and medical research and comedy shows. She also dabbles in coding, photography and graphic design.

Before working as a journalist, she used to poke glowing nematodes and wrangle mice for a living at the University of Florida. There, she developed an interest for science and a disinterest for the lab bench.

She dreams of writing and producing her own science television show one day. In the meantime, you can find her shooting video, making Lego mosaics or appreciating rainbow-colored things.
